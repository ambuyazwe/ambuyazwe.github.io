# 3D & Drawing

Welcome to this section of the gallery, featuring an array of hand-drawn projects, handmade packaging, and 3D renders created using MAYA 3D and Blender 3D. The packaging projects displayed here were developed as part of my tertiary education in graphic design. The hand-drawn artworks include personal pieces and contributions to Inktober over the years.

Specializing in drawing and character design, I am also highly skilled in product packaging design. Browse through this collection to witness my proficiency and creativity across different mediums.
